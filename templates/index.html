Face Recognition Attendance System

It can be very tedious for a teacher to take attendance in every class, not to mention how time consuming it is. A simple solution for this would be a smart face recognition based attendance system, which would recognise the faces of the students in the class for determining attendance of students, allowing teachers to spend more time on teaching.
